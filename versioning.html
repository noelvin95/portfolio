Artifact versioning — change people can trust

In a governed catalogue, the meaning of a term doesn't stay still — it gets refined, corrected, re-approved. People need to see what changed, when, and why, without ever losing the old truth. This was about designing how versioning works inside IBM Knowledge Catalog.

The problem we were really solving
